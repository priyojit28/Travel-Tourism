CHITRAKOTA FALLS

The Chitrakote Falls is a natural waterfall located to the west of Jagdalpur, in Bastar district in the Indian state of Chhattisgarh on the Indravati River. It is located at a distance of 38 kilometres (24 mi) to the west of Jagdalpur.Hailed as the Niagara Falls of India, Chitrakot Waterfalls in Chhattisgarh is located to the west of Jagdalpur in Bastar district. The Indravati river, originating in Kalahandi region of Odisha, falls from a height of 29m to form this waterfall.
